Study Instructions - Please read carefully

Part 1
  1. Once you have read this section and clicked on proceed, you will see a selection of 30 artworks. You can interact with them, e.g., through clicks, and have a browse. Out of these 30, please select 5 to 10 artworks (never choose more than 10, this could result in an error!) that you would like to see more of. You can do so by clicking an artwork and hitting the select button (or deselect shoud you wish to revoke one of your choices). Once you have chosen 5 to 10 artworks, you can click on the "Show me more" button in the top right corner of the page.
  2. You will now get to see another set of artworks. Please look at them carefully and keep things in mind that you might have noticed when comparing them to your first selection. You are again asked to select 5 to 10 artworks of which you would like to see more of.
  3. This process repeats for a number of rounds.
Part 2

This part follows the same procedure as Part 1. Please look at the artworks carefully and make mental notes about any peculiarities you might or might not notice.

After Part 2, we kindly ask you to fill in a post-study questionnaire.

Thank you and enjoy the study, and the artworks of course!

All artworks are protected by copyright laws, it is illegal to download, copy, share, or use them for any other purpose than this study.

When browsing, please always use the "Go Back" button in the upper left corner of the study's website and don't use your browsers back button.

Please finish in a single sitting (approximate time of study: 20 minutes).
